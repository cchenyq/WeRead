Our mission

We are a virtual platform helping youth who are immigrants and refugees learn English and integrate into Canada by improving their literacy. We believe literacy is important for the following reasons:

26% of immigrants to Canada are youth

ESL education funding per student is decreasing

Language barriers and lack of access to information keep immigrant children excluded from Canadian society

Meanwhile, 85% of youth under 18 who speak English as a second language learned English through reading

2. Get reading
